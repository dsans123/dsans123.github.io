Stats

Stats in Elden ring refer to the strengths and weaknesses of your character. you can increase these stats by levelling up. Leveling is done by killing enemeis who will drop runes which you can then spend to increase your stats. Melina, an NPC in Elden Ring, will come to you at the start of the game and will offer you the power to turn your runes into strength. The game features 8 main stats. Vigor is the attribute that determines your health points aswell as your fire resistance and immunity. Mind affects your FP which is the attribute which determines how much mana you have. Endurance determines your stamina bar aswell as how heavy your character can be without switching between light, medium and heavy rolling. Strength is the attribute that is required to weeild heavy armaments as well as influencing the players physical/strike/slash/pierce defense and provides damage scaling for weapons that utilize the stat. Dexterity is in the same boat as strenght in which it is used for wielding advanced armaments as well as scaling the damage of those armaments. Intelligence and Faith are stats that are used to either casst glintstone sorceries or sacred incantations, the stat also allows the sorceries damage to scale with the stat. Lastly Arcane is the stat that influences the payers Holy and Death resistance, provides damage scaling for weapons that use the stat as well as providing scaling for the poison, bleed, sleep and madness status effect build ups.
