Lore

The Tarnishsed

The playable character of Elden Ring. The Tarnishsed are a individuals who lost the grace of the Erdtree and were banished from the Lands Between by Queen Marika following the breaking of the Elden Ring. However the Tarnished were beckoned by lost grace, a fragment of divine light from the Erdtree, to once again return to the Lands Between where they would restore order and become Elden Lord.

Godrick the Grafted

Godrick the Grafted is the first demi-god shardbearer ( bosses that provide you grat runes ) you encounter in Elden Ring. Although he is not a mandatory boss, you will need two great runes to access Leyndell Royal Capital and progress the story. Godrick is the ruler of Stormveil castle which bars the way into Liurnia of the Lakes. He is a descendant of Godfrey and Queen Marika the current holder of the Elden Ring. His main source of strength comes from grafting the arms and legs of his soldiers onto his own body though one of his most powerful weapon is the Dragon head grafted onto his right arm

Rennala Queen of the Full Moon

Rennala is another of the shardbearers you encounter on your journey across the Lands Between. Like Godrick, beating Rennala is not required to progress the story, doing so unlocks the ability to respec and is required if you wish to progress to a certain ending. In her youth Rennala was a prominent sorcerer and became the master of Raya Lucaria the academy of magic. She married Radagon, champion of Queen Marika in which marriage they had 3 children. Radahn, Rykard and Ranni. When Radagon left Rennala to marry Queen Marika and become the second Elden Lord he left her with the Great Rune of the Unborn in which the Tarnished hunts down.

Starscourge Radahn

Starscourge Radahn is a sharbearer boss encountered in Caelid. The son of Rennala and Radagon, as well as the demigod stepson of Queen marika. Radahn is regarded as the mightiest hero of the demigods, and was one of the strongest participants of the Shattering. Radahn was enamored by the Elden Lord Godfrey. As a result Radahn decorated all of his armour with lions in order to emulate his hero. Radahn grew huge due to his demigod stature and not wanting to abondin his trusty steed Leonard, Radahn set out to learn gravity magic in Selia. Mastering this magic allowed Radahn to manipulate the forces of gravity as well as challenge the very starts themselves. Radahn conquered the starts and saved Selia in the process. This earned him the moniker Starscourge.

Morgott the Omen King

Morgott the Omen King is the main boss found in Leyndell Royal Capital and must be defeated in order to progress the story and enter the mountaintops of the giants. Morgott is the demigod son of Queen Marika and Elden Lord Godfrey. When Queen Marika shattered the Elden Ring, Morgott claimed one of the Great Runes. He claimed Leyndell for himself and ddefended it and the Erdtree against his demigod siblings. Despite protecting Leyndell he found the Erdtree would not admit him to claim the title of Elden Lord. He vowed to protect the Erdtree so he stayed in Leyndell as the Erdtrees protector. During this time he took up the name of Margit the Fell Omen in order to stop any champions who wished for the title of Elden Lord.

Fire Giant

The Fire Giant is a Legend Boss in Elden Ring. He is a required boss to beat in order to progress the story. He is the last known survivor of the war against the giants. He has a face on his chest which spews fire. Not much is known about this boss. Once Marika realised the flames of the giants forge would never die Marika marked him with a curse to ever tend the flames. The tarnished wish to burn the cursed thorns of the Erdtree in order to gain entry and become Elden Lord so the tarnished slays the Fire Giant to use the giant's forge.

Maliketh the Black Blade

Beast Clergyman and Maliketh the Black Blade is a two phase boss fight where Maliketh reveals himself once you do enough damage to the Beast Clergyman. The fight is mandatory to progress towards the end of the game. Maliketh is Queen Marikas shadow-bound beast. He had the task of guarding the Rune of Death howver his failure in doing so led to the Night of the Black Knives where a group of assassins infused the Runes of Death into their blades in order to be able to kill even gods. After Malikeths failure he infused the Rune of Death into his own skin in order to never lose it again.

Godfrey, The First Elden Lord

Godfrey, First Elden Lord and Horah Loux, Warrior is a two phase Legend boss in Elden Ring. The boss is mandatory to progress towards thge end of the game. Godfrey was Queen Marika's first consort. Before earning this title he was know ans Hoarah Loux, a bloodfirsty warrior and the chieftains of the badlands. He was gifted the Beast Regent Serosh, a lion, upon his back to contol his violent urges. After fighting and winning multiple wars for Queen Marika, she deprived him of grace and casted him out from the lands between marking him as the first tarnished.

Radagon of the Golden Order

Radagon of the Golden Order is the second to last mandatory boss in Elden Ring. Radagon ia a tall god wielding the hammer that shattered the Elden Ring. He was previously married to Rennala but married Queen Marika after Godfrey was exiled from the Lands Between. It is revealed that Radagon and Marika share the same body but do not share the same will which is apparent from Radagons efforts to try and repair the Elden Ring after Marika shatterd it . Killing Radagon will reveal a second phase to the boss fight where you must fight the Elden Beast

The Elden Beast

Not much is known about the Elden Beast. However we do know that the Elden Beast is the true form of the Elden Ring acting as a vassol of the Greater Will (gods acting on the outside of the Lands Between) and is found in the Elden Throne. The Elden Beast was sent to the Lands Between long before the beginning of the game through a golden star. Defeating the boss allows you to erpair the Elden Ring and will trigger the rolling of the credits and signals the end of the games main story.
